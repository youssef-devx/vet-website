About us

We Care as Much as You Do

Do you consider your pet a member of the family? We do.

Don’t they deserve the best medical attention available?

At Post Road Veterinary Clinic, your pet’s health and safety is our primary concern. Our highly experienced staff and compassionate veterinarians effectively diagnose and treat your animal companion quickly, but carefully. From primping and preening to delicate surgical procedures, we treat your pet the way you would: with personal attention and a soft touch, because we care as much as you do.

Whether your dog’s teeth need a cleaning, or it’s time for your cat’s regular check-up, we have comprehensive facilities and a professional staff to solve all your pet’s problems.

And for those times when you must leave town without your pet, trust our boarding facilities for the safest and most comfortable home-away-from-home.

From life-threatening emergencies to mundane grooming issues, no matter is insignificant. Contact us today and take advantage of our flexible daytime or evening appointments. We strive to make premium veterinary services convenient. Give the animals in your family the care they need, without conflicts to your hectic schedule.
